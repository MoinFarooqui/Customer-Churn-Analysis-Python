# 📈 Business Insights

## Executive Summary

The exploratory analysis highlights several customer characteristics and service-related factors that are associated with customer churn. These insights can support customer retention strategies and improve business decision-making.

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Customer Churn Distribution

* The majority of customers remained with the company.
* A significant portion of customers left the service, indicating opportunities to improve customer retention.

---

### 2. Senior Citizens

* Senior citizens showed a relatively higher tendency to churn compared to non-senior customers.
* This customer segment may benefit from targeted retention initiatives.

---

### 3. Contract Type

* Customers with month-to-month contracts experienced the highest churn.
* Long-term contracts showed stronger customer retention.

---

### 4. Customer Tenure

* Customers with shorter tenure were more likely to churn.
* Customers who stayed longer were generally more loyal.

---

### 5. Payment Method

* Electronic check users appeared to have a higher churn rate than customers using other payment methods.
* Payment preferences may influence customer retention.

---

### 6. Internet Services

* Churn behavior varied across different internet service categories.
* Service quality and customer experience may influence retention.

---

### 7. Streaming Services

* Streaming TV and Streaming Movies showed different customer adoption patterns.
* These services alone did not appear to be the primary drivers of churn.

---

### 8. Security & Support Services

Customers subscribed to:

* Online Security
* Tech Support
* Device Protection

generally demonstrated stronger customer retention compared to customers without these services.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Encourage customers to move from month-to-month contracts to longer-term plans.
* Improve onboarding experiences for new customers.
* Promote value-added services such as Online Security and Tech Support.
* Review customer experience for electronic check users.
* Develop targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customer segments.

---

## Overall Conclusion

The exploratory data analysis identified meaningful relationships between customer demographics, subscribed services, contract types, payment methods, and churn behavior. These insights can help businesses design effective customer retention strategies and provide a strong foundation for predictive churn modeling in future projects.
